# Connection 

| L293D || left / right |
|-:|-|-|
| DIR | < | red - right |
| DIR | < | orange - left |
| GND | < | brown |

##

| L293D || right - stop |
|-:|-|-|
| DIR | < | yellow  |
| PWM | < | orange |

##

| L293D || left - stop |
|-:|-|-|
| DIR | < | red |
| PWM | < | orange |
